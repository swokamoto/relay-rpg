# ⚡ Relay RPG — Quick Start

Get playing in under five minutes.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Character

Run these four commands once — they carry across all stories:

- `/name "Character Name"`
- `/conviction "What drives them"`
- `/talent "What they're good at"`
- `/quirk "What makes them interesting or complicated"`

---

## Step 2: Find a Story

Use `/hooks` to browse open stories and click **Join Adventure** on a post. You can only be in one story at a time.

---

## Step 3: Take a Turn

When it's your turn, use `/turn` and write your narration. Every turn has three parts:

1. **Acknowledge the previous roll** — what did the last result mean for the scene?
2. **Describe your action** — end on something with genuine stakes
3. **Roll** — `/turn` handles this automatically

> ⚠️ Your action must have real stakes. Success and failure should pull the story in *different directions* — if they'd lead to the same place, find a riskier action.

---

## Dice at a Glance

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 2–3  | 💥 Critical Failure — +2 failures |
| 4–5  | ❌ Failure — +1 failure |
| 6–8  | 🤔 Partial — builds tension (3 in a row = auto-failure) |
| 9–10 | ✅ Success — +1 success |
| 11–12 | ⭐ Critical Success — +2 successes |

Scene ends at **3 successes** or **3 failures**. There are 4 Acts total.

---

## Traits

You have 3 traits (conviction, talent, quirk) — each usable **once per story** for **+2 to a roll**. Weave the trait into your narration when you spend it. Save them for moments that actually matter.

---

## Key Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| `/hooks` | Browse open stories |
| `/turn` | Take your turn + roll |
| `/truth` | Declare a scene detail (once per act) |
| `/leave` | Exit the current story |
| `/transition` | Write act transition (when you end an act) |
| `/epilogue` | Post your character's ending |
