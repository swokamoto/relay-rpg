# ⚡ Relay RPG — Quick Start

You're not trying to win — you're trying to leave the next person something worth inheriting.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Character

Run these four commands once — they carry across all stories:

- `/name "Character Name"`
- `/conviction "What drives them"`
- `/talent "What they're good at"`
- `/quirk "What makes them interesting or complicated"`

The richest characters have traits that pull against each other. A character who is *loyal, stealthy, and terrible at lying* will face more interesting decisions than one who is simply good at everything.

---

## Step 2: Join a Story

Use `/hooks` to browse open stories and click **Join Adventure**. You can only be in one story at a time.

Want to run a story yourself? Use `/post` to create one, then `/begin` to open it once players have joined.

---

## How a Story Flows

Each story has **4 Acts**. An act ends when the group reaches **3 successes** or **3 failures**.

Inside each act:

1. **Declare a Truth** — Use `/truth` once per act to establish a fact about the scene. It becomes canon instantly. Do this at the start of an act to set the stage.
2. **Take turns** — Use `/turn` and write your narration. Acknowledge the last roll, describe your action, end on genuine stakes.
3. **Write the transition** — Whoever lands the final roll of an act uses `/transition` to bridge to the next scene using the formula: *"[result], but [complication], therefore [next setup]."*

After Act III comes the **Climax** — no transition, just the finale. The Host writes it with `/finale`.

---

## Taking a Turn

Every `/turn` has three parts, written together:

1. **Acknowledge the previous roll** — what did that result mean for the scene?
2. **Describe your action** — be specific, end on something with real stakes
3. **Roll** — `/turn` handles this automatically

> Your action needs genuine risk. If success and failure would lead to the same place, find a riskier action.

---

## Dice at a Glance

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 2–3  | 💥 Critical Failure — +2 failures |
| 4–5  | ❌ Failure — +1 failure |
| 6–8  | 🤔 Partial — builds tension (3 in a row = auto-failure) |
| 9–10 | ✅ Success — +1 success |
| 11–12 | ⭐ Critical Success — +2 successes |

The most common result is a partial. The game is built around complications — learn to write interesting ones.

---

## Traits

Your 3 traits (conviction, talent, quirk) can each be used **once per story** for **+2 to a roll**. Weave the trait into your narration when you spend it. Save them for moments that actually matter — the first roll of the game is too early.

## All Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| `/hooks` | Browse open stories |
| `/post` | Create a new story (Host) |
| `/begin` | Open your story to play (Host) |
| `/turn` | Take your turn + roll |
| `/truth` | Declare a scene fact (once per act) |
| `/transition` | Bridge to the next act (whoever ends the act) |
| `/finale` | Write the climax ending (Host) |
| `/epilogue` | Post your character's ending |
| `/leave` | Exit the current story |
